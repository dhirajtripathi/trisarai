# Auto Insurance Policy - QuickCover

## Coverage: Comprehensive
- **Covered**: Fire, theft, vandalism, falling objects, natural disasters.
- **Deductible**: $500.
- **Exclusion**: Damage caused by intentional acts.

## Coverage: Collision
- **Covered**: Impact with another vehicle or object.
- **Deductible**: $500.
- **Specific Rule**: If the vehicle is parked and hit by an unknown driver (Hit and Run), details of the police report are required.

## Coverage: Glass
- **Covered**: Windshield, side windows, rear window.
- **Deductible**: $50 (Special Low Deductible for Glass).
- **Limit**: $1000 per incident.

## Exclusions (General)
- **Off-Road Usage**: Any damage occurring while driving on non-paved roads or competitive racing tracks is **NOT COVERED**.
- **Ride-Sharing**: Damage occurring while the vehicle is being used for commercial ride-sharing (without specific endorsement) is **NOT COVERED**.
- **Wear and Tear**: Mechanical breakdown or rust is **NOT COVERED**.
